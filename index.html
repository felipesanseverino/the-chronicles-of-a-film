i photograph the places i pass through and the moments i live. on film when i can, on digital when i can't wait.

this site is the archive: rolls, chapters, and the frames in between. nothing here is heavily edited. sometimes i crop, but the colors or the lack of them are made in camera. no after-shot adjustments. just the record, kept slowly.
